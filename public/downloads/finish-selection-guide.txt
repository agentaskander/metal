AMERICA'S PANEL FAB
FINISH SELECTION GUIDE

Common finish paths:

PVDF / Kynar-style finishes
- Premium commercial and architectural finish path.
- Good for long-life roof and wall panel applications.

Silicone-modified polyester
- Durable and economical finish option.
- Good for broad commercial and industrial coverage.

Matte architectural colors
- Lower-glare modern commercial appearance.
- Useful for multifamily, office, retail, and campus environments.

Cool roof whites and grays
- Reflective finish path for heat-aware projects.
- Useful in high-sun regions such as Arizona and Texas.

Coastal and specialty considerations
- Coastal projects may require closer review of finish, substrate, fastener, and edge details.

Final finish recommendations depend on project location, exposure, budget, color requirements, and warranty path.
