AMERICAN SUPER PANEL
FINISH SELECTION GUIDE

Use this guide to choose a finish path for roof panels, siding panels, trim,
flashing, fascia, soffit, and accessories.

Finish families:

Metallic utility finishes
- Examples: Galvalume, galvanized.
- Useful for shops, warehouses, agricultural buildings, storage buildings, and
  practical commercial packages.

Primer-ready / Rezibond-style finishes
- Weathered zinc / gunmetal design intent.
- Useful for selected architectural, industrial, and interior-facing design
  conversations.
- Confirm primer-ready availability and project suitability before release.

SMP painted finishes
- Durable, economical painted finish path.
- Useful for broad commercial, agricultural, industrial, and residential support
  work.

PVDF / Kynar-style finishes
- Premium architectural finish conversation.
- Useful for owner standards, commercial visibility, campus work, and long-life
  exterior appearance expectations.

Matte architectural finishes
- Low-glare modern finish path.
- Useful for shops, retail, offices, multifamily, and visible trim or siding.

Cool roof light colors
- Examples: Polar White, light grays, selected reflective colors.
- Useful for heat-aware projects and light-color roof conversations.

Specialty accents
- Examples: Copper Penny, wood grain, custom color coordination.
- Useful for fascia, soffit, trim, accent panels, and high-visibility details.

Finish selection checklist:
- Project state
- Roof, wall, trim, flashing, or accessory use
- Desired color names
- Finish family preference
- Panel profile
- Gauge and substrate preference, if known
- Sample request needed
- Owner, architect, or contractor approval required
- Trim and flashing color match required
- Alternate color acceptable if availability changes

Important note:
Screen colors are planning references only. Final finish recommendations depend
on project location, exposure, budget, color requirements, coating system,
substrate, gauge, warranty path, and current material availability.
