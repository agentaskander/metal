AMERICAS PANEL FAB
PLAN UPLOAD INSTRUCTIONS

Accepted upload types:

- PDF plans
- Drawings
- Structural documents
- DWG or DXF exports
- JPG or PNG site/reference images

Maximum combined upload size: 100MB.

Recommended file naming:

ProjectName_State_SheetType_Date.pdf

Examples:

Warehouse-AZ_RoofPlan_2026-06-17.pdf
School-CA_Elevations_2026-06-17.pdf
Municipal-FL_Structural_2026-06-17.pdf

Include:

- Roof plans
- Wall elevations
- Details
- Structural sheets
- Finish/color requirements
- Bid due date or desired completion date

Do not upload sensitive owner financial information, payment data, or unrelated personal documents.
