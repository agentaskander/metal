AMERICAN SUPER PANEL
SUBMITTAL PACKAGE GUIDE

A project submittal package may include:

- Panel profile information
- Material and finish notes
- Fastener path notes
- Trim and flashing package notes
- Color/finish selections
- Warranty path notes
- Project-specific review comments

Best-fit projects:

- Commercial roofing
- Metal siding panels
- Agricultural buildings
- Warehouses and industrial buildings
- Multifamily exterior packages
- Residential shops, garages, and accessory buildings

Submittal content depends on final project scope, selected system, finish path, and assembly requirements.
